What is Cyber Secruity And How to
Be Secure On The Internet?

Cyber security is the practice of protecting your digital assets from unauthorized access, use, or damage. It involves using various tools, techniques, and strategies to prevent cyberattacks and minimize their impact. The internet is a great resource for learning, entertainment, and communication. But it also exposes you to various risks and threats. Hackers, scammers, and cybercriminals can try to steal your personal information, infect your devices with malware, or compromise your accounts. They can also use the internet to spread misinformation, propaganda, or hate speech.

To be secure on the internet, you need to follow some basic guidelines and best practices. Here are some of them:

  1. Use strong and unique passwords for your online accounts. Change them regularly and don’t share them with anyone.
  2. Use a password manager to store and manage your passwords securely.
  3. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for your accounts whenever possible. This adds an extra layer of security by requiring a code or a device to log in.
  4. Use a reputable antivirus software and keep it updated. Scan your devices regularly for malware and viruses.
  5. Avoid clicking on suspicious links or opening attachments from unknown sources. They could contain malware or phishing attempts.
  6. Be careful about what you share online. Don’t post sensitive or personal information that could be used against you or to impersonate you.
  7. Be aware of the signs of phishing and social engineering. These are techniques used by cybercriminals to trick you into giving up your information or access to your accounts.
  8. Use a VPN (virtual private network) to encrypt your internet traffic and hide your online activity from prying eyes.
  9. Use secure and trusted websites and apps. Look for the padlock icon and the HTTPS protocol in the address bar.
  10. Educate yourself about the latest cyber threats and trends. Stay informed and alert.

Cyber security is not only a technical issue but also a human one. You need to be responsible and vigilant when using the internet. You also need to respect the rights and privacy of others online. By following these tips, you can enjoy the benefits of the internet while staying safe and secure.
